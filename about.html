About Ejoshi

It started with a family.

Ejoshi is named after two sons and a family name — a father’s decision to make sure his kids, and every family like his, face the AI era with a clear way to learn.

The Story

Why Ejoshi exists.

Founder Ejoshi Li built Ejoshi because AI is rewriting the world, and refused to let his kids — and every family like his — face that change without a way to learn it.

Not through fear. Not through hype. Not through jargon. Through simple lessons, real examples, useful prompts, and a voice that feels like someone walking with you.

The last two technology waves — dot-com and smartphones — left a lot of people behind. Their work changed underfoot. Their kids inherited the gap. AI is bigger than either of those waves, and it’s already moving. Ejoshi is the place we’re building so the next generation, and the one before it, doesn’t have to stand still.

Ejoshi is made for the ones who move different.

What We Believe

AI is not for the few. It is for the ones who decide to learn.

Wisdom over hype. We teach AI as a craft, not a magic spell. We tell the truth about what these tools can do, and what they can’t.

Practical over performative. Every lesson begins with a real-life problem and ends with a real-life next step.

Warmth over distance. We sound like a wise older sibling who happens to be from the future — calm, patient, never preachy.

Access over exclusivity. Ejoshi is built so curious people — anywhere, in any walk of life — can walk in and learn. Free to access. No paywall. No gatekeeping. The future should not be locked behind one.

Family-built, not corporate. Ejoshi is named after a family. Every decision asks: would a thoughtful parent be proud of this?

What Ejoshi Is Building

Three things, woven together.

Ejoshi isn’t a course or a newsletter or a brand. It’s all three. We’re building toward a place that lives in your inbox, on your screen, in your classroom, and on your shelf.

Pillar · 01

A learning platform

Courses, tutorials, prompt libraries, workshops, guides, and practical AI lessons — being built piece by piece. All in plain language. Free to access. No paywall.

Pillar · 02

A community

A global, intergenerational space for people learning AI together — students, parents, educators, builders, lifelong learners.

Pillar · 03

A lifestyle brand

Apparel and merch people wear because they chose to learn. The brand worn quietly, by the ones who move different.

We’re not pretending all three are finished. They’re what Ejoshi is building toward — and the first version of each is what you’ll see roll out as we build in public.

Who It’s For

Anyone who decides to learn AI instead of fear it.

The student trying to study smarter without losing their own voice. The parent who wants to help with homework but doesn’t know where to start. The educator who wants AI in the classroom without losing the soul of the work.

The small-shop operator who could use a tireless helper. The late-starter who thought the future already left without them. The lifelong learner who walks into every new era a little curious and a little brave.

If any of those sound like you — Ejoshi is yours.
