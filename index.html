About Ejoshi

A name with a reason.

Ejoshi is named after my two sons and our family last name.

I started Ejoshi because the world was changing faster than schools, faster than most people's jobs, faster than most families were ready for. I didn't want my kids — or anyone's kids, or anyone trying to keep up — to feel like they'd already missed the moment.

They hadn't. You haven't. AI is a tool. Tools can be learned. We teach how — for free, in plain language, starting now.

Our logo is a book with a lightbulb. The book is learning. The lightbulb is the idea that follows. AI is where they meet.

"Made for the ones who move different."

Who it's for

Everyone, starting with students.

Ejoshi is built in phases. Phase 1 is for students 13–18 — because the earlier you learn to work with AI, the further it takes you.

Phase 1 — Now

Students 13–18

You're already using AI for homework. We teach you how to use it well — so you learn more, think harder, and actually get somewhere.

Phase 2 — Coming next

Parents and teachers

You want to know if this is safe, honest, and worth their time. We're building for you too — guides, resources, and a community you can trust.

Phase 3 — On the roadmap

Working adults

You don't need to understand how AI works underneath. You need to know how to use it for your life, your job, and your family.
